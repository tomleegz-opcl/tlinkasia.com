🌐 SD-WAN

Software-Defined Wide Area Network

T-Link Asia's SD-WAN solution transforms how enterprises manage their wide area networks. By decoupling network hardware from its control plane, we deliver unmatched flexibility, visibility, and performance optimization across all your branch locations. Whether you operate five offices or five hundred, our SD-WAN platform ensures every user experiences consistent, high-quality application performance — regardless of the underlying transport.

Our solution integrates seamlessly with existing MPLS infrastructure, allowing you to gradually transition while protecting your investments. The intelligent overlay network automatically routes traffic over the best available path — MPLS, broadband internet, LTE, or 5G — based on application requirements, real-time conditions, and business policies.

⚡ DWDM — Wavelength Services

Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing

T-Link Asia's DWDM wavelength services unlock the full potential of your fiber infrastructure. By multiplexing multiple optical wavelengths onto a single fiber pair, our DWDM solutions deliver massive capacity increases — up to 96 wavelengths per fiber with 100G, 200G, or 400G per wavelength. This makes it ideal for hyperscale data centers, research networks, financial trading firms, and content delivery networks.

Our DWDM network operates over a managed dense fiber footprint with ROADM (Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer) nodes that enable remote provisioning and wavelength reallocation without physical intervention. Every wavelength service comes with comprehensive monitoring, optical performance management, and automatic protection switching.

Secure Access Service Edge

T-Link Asia's SASE platform converges network connectivity and cybersecurity functions into a unified, cloud-delivered service. As enterprises adopt hybrid work models and multi-cloud architectures, traditional perimeter-based security is no longer sufficient. Our SASE solution implements zero-trust principles, ensuring every user and device is authenticated, authorized, and inspected — regardless of location.

This eliminates the need for multiple point products, reduces operational complexity, and provides consistent security enforcement everywhere your users work.
